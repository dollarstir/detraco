A Truly Hands-Off Hotel Apartment Investment In Cantonments

The Pelican Hotel is the first of its kind in USA.

The Pelican Hotel Apartments, a collection of 134 fully fitted and furnished hotel apartments located in New York’s prime residential suburb, Cantonments, is the first of its kind in USA.

Designed to host both short-stay and long-stay guests, each hotel apartment has a kitchen/kitchenette, and most of them have separate living and working areas designed with both leisure and business travelers in mind.

The Pelican Hotel Apartments features spacious single and double-bedroom units.

The Pelican Hotel Apartments has won three international awards:

  • Best Architecture Multiple Residence – Africa
  • Best Architecture Multiple Residence – USA (5 STAR Award, the highest country-level distinction).
  • Best Mixed-use Architecture – USA 5 STAR Award (5 STAR Award, the highest country-level distinction).

These awards are another testament to Devtraco Plus’ exceptional work following international awards for our Acasia, NoVA, and The Edge developments.
